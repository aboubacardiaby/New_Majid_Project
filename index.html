About Our Community

Our Mission

The Gambian Muslim Community in Minnesota is dedicated to preserving our Islamic faith and Gambian cultural heritage while integrating into the broader Minnesota community. We strive to provide spiritual guidance, educational opportunities, and social support for Gambian Muslims and their families.

Our Values

  • Faith and Spirituality
  • Community Unity
  • Education and Learning
  • Service to Others
  • Cultural Preservation

Our Services

Prayer Services

Daily congregational prayers, Jummah prayers, and special occasion prayers including Eid celebrations.

Islamic Education

Quran classes for children and adults, Islamic studies, Arabic language instruction, and religious guidance.

Community Support

Counseling services, family support, assistance for new immigrants, and community welfare programs.

Cultural Events

Gambian cultural celebrations, Islamic holidays, community gatherings, and interfaith dialogue events.

Marriage & Family

Islamic marriage ceremonies, family counseling, youth programs, and life event celebrations.

Zakat & Charity

Zakat collection and distribution, charitable giving coordination, and community assistance programs.
